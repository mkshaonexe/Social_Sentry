WHAT TO DO:
1. Type "SocialSentry" in the search box to filter only our app's logs
2. Clear old logs by clicking the 🗑️ trash icon
3. Open Facebook on emulator and scroll
4. Watch the logs appear in real-time!

WHAT LOGS YOU SHOULD SEE WHEN SCROLLING FACEBOOK:

✅ Good - Scroll limiter is working:
   D/SocialSentry: Facebook detected, scroll limiter enabled, tracking...
   D/SocialSentry: Facebook scroll limiter: ⏱️ Started tracking scroll time
   V/SocialSentry: Facebook scroll limiter: Duration = 10s

   V/SocialSentry: Facebook scroll limiter: Duration = 60s
   D/SocialSentry: Facebook scroll limiter: 🚨 1 minute threshold reached

❌ Bad - Scroll limiter NOT detecting scrolls:
   D/SocialSentry: Facebook detected but scroll limiter is disabled
   (No other messages)
